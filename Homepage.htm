Through collaboration, the client can be empowered to take ownership of their products: capable of independently creating new ones.

About me

I was born 30 years ago in wonderful Foligno, alongside the hills of Umbria, in Italy 🇮🇹.

After my high school diploma (science 🧬) and a brief time at the Politecnico di Milano (communication design course 🖼️, carpentry training in the side 🪚), I spent 8 years in Bozen-Bolzano: at the border between Italy and Austria 🥨.

There I obtained a bachelor degree in design and art from the trilingual Free University of Bozen-Bolzano.
I spent my time in the Alps working and studying, starting my graphic design practice in 2016 🏔️.

Following my thesis research about communicating disposable plastic's production, consumption and recycling to the city, in 2021 I was selected to co-found the New European Bauhaus of the Mountains.

Currently I live and work in Foligno, Umbria, Italy.

Opening design to the people ultimately means to open society 🏳️‍🌈.
